About Simfluence

Dubai, United Arab Emirates

Simfluence is an AI-powered, continuously learning platform that predicts the outcome of creator-driven campaigns before a single penny is spent. No wasted resources. No guesswork. Just insight — delivered with a level of accuracy the industry has never seen before.

What we do

Influencer marketing has a fundamental problem: brands commit significant budgets to campaigns without any reliable forecast of what those campaigns will actually deliver. The result is endemic — 68% of influencer campaigns underperform their expectations, not because the creators were wrong choices, but because nobody ran a proper forecast before launch.

Simfluence solves this at the source. Input your brief. Select your creators. Run the simulation. In under two minutes you receive probabilistic forecasts for CTR, CPM, CVR and ROAS — complete with P10, P50 and P90 confidence bands — before you commit a single pound, dirham, or dollar.

How the intelligence works

Our platform is built on two proprietary scoring systems. The Creator Performance Index (CPI) quantifies a creator's predicted ability to drive commercial outcomes for a specific brief — not their follower count, their results. The Genre Fit Index (GFI) measures the alignment between a creator's native audience and your campaign's target genre and sub-mode.

Every simulation run on Simfluence feeds our learning model. When brands return after campaigns to log their actual results, the platform compares predicted against actual, identifies the delta, and refines its coefficients. The more campaigns run through Simfluence, the more accurate the forecasts become. This is the compounding edge no static tool can replicate.

Who we built this for

Simfluence is designed for marketing leaders, game publishers, studios, and agencies who are accountable for campaign ROI and tired of committing budget on instinct. Our platform covers 18 content genres, 53 sub-modes, and 12 platforms — including YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, Twitch, and Kick — with particular depth in MENA, Europe, and North America.

Our commitment

We believe the influencer marketing industry deserves better data. Not historical reports produced after the budget is gone, but forward intelligence produced before a single decision is made. Simfluence is built on the principle that brands deserve to know what they are buying before they buy it.

We do not take commission on deals. We do not monetise creator relationships. We charge a flat subscription for access to the platform and nothing else. Your data, your campaigns, and your creator relationships remain entirely yours.
