Platform

Four tools.
Zero guesswork.

Creator Discovery
Find creators by what they will deliver, not by how many follow them. Our database grows continuously — new creators added across every genre, platform and region as the influencer landscape evolves. Every result ranked by CPI in real time.

CPI & GFI Scoring
Two scores that do what follower count never could. The Creator Performance Index predicts commercial outcomes. The Genre Fit Index measures audience alignment to your campaign territory. Together they make the right shortlist obvious.
0–100 scale · 90% confidence · live data
AI Persona Matching
Every creator carries an audience archetype — the way their followers think, buy and respond to a call to action. Simfluence maps 100+ genre-native personas to your brand narrative so your message lands with the right psychology, not just the right demographic.
